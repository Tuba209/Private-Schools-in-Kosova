Mehmet Akif School is founded in February 2000 by a group of parents and educators to contribute to the education of Kosovo. Mehmet Akif School offers a challenging education to students focusing both on academic achievement and personal growth, regardless of religion, race, nationality or sex. More than 90% of the graduating seniors enter universities and institutions of higher learning worldwide. Mehmet Akif School is committed to a quality of education that fosters international understanding and friendship, and which provides students with a clear sense and understanding of humane and democratic values.

ALG

Loyola is a private shcool in Kosovo. Loyola Campus is a Great Place to Learn The Association "Loyola-Gymnasium" (ALG) is a politically independent and non-profit educational institution (NGO). In our Loyola-Fillore (elementary school from 1st to 5th grade), our Loyola-Gymnasium (from 6th to 12th grade), our Loyola-Konvikt (boarding school from 06th grade) and in our Loyola-Profesionale (vocational training center) we apply an open, people-oriented hollistic pedagogy. This is strongly based on the pedagogy of the Jesuits (Ignatian pedagogy). In addition to the Kosovar curriculum of the Ministry of Education, ALG offers various additional educational programs and extracurricular activities. Our goal is to create a young, self-confident, modern and self (critical) thinking civil society. The youth of today will soon be the next decision makers and change agents of the country and beyond.
